about us

GFORM IS A MARKET-LEADING TENANT AND COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE MANAGEMENT SPECIALIST.

We work with some of Germany’s foremost retail real estate investors and developers and our managed portfolio includes some of Germany’s leading retail centres.

We fully understand the importance of the partnership between landlord and tenant, and we have an established track record of building and maintaining tenant relationships that deliver proven results for both parties.

GFORM is part of the Greenman Group and manages the €1.13bn Greenman OPEN portfolio of grocery-anchored retail real estate located across Germany.

HISTORY

Greenman formed

Management of auto centre investment properties in Germany for a volume of €10m

Management of grocery anchored retail investment properties in Germany for a volume of €76m

Opens Berlin office

€461m portfolio of real estate in investment properties under management following a buy & hold strategy

Formation of the Greenman Group

Development of ESG framework for the properties under management including a focus on reducing their carbon footprint

Formation of GFORM to focus on tenant relationships and German growth
